What is CBM?

CBM (Cubic Meter) is a unit of measurement that expresses the volume of a cargo. It is used in the logistics industry to calculate cargo costs.

Formula: Length × Width × Height ÷ 1,000,000

Volumetric Weight

Volumetric weight is the theoretical weight calculated based on the volume of the cargo. Cargo companies base their pricing on whichever is higher: actual weight or volumetric weight.

Formula: CBM × 167 (for air cargo)
