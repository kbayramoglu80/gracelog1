About Us

Grace International Logistics is an innovative structure that serves its solution partners and customers in the fields of logistics, supply chain and transportation with its expert and young staff, backed by Turkey's strong infrastructure.

We continuously develop our knowledge and skills to provide timely and effective solutions to our customers' needs and expectations.

Our logistics company specializes in air freight transportation. We offer the highest quality service to ensure your cargo reaches its destinations safely, economically and efficiently.

With our expert team and knowledge, we can respond to all your sea freight transportation needs. We can also offer special solutions for Project transportation and Bulk cargo.

As a logistics service provider partner, we create services and solutions beyond the needs of all our customers in the international market

Our general perspective aims to achieve excellence in operational quality and efficiency while producing solutions for the development of business processes.

Our main goals are to create a quality and fast solution partnership where we can create transparent, reliable and long-term special services with all our customers.
