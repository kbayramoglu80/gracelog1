Industry Insights

Logistics Industry Overview

The logistics industry is one of the fundamental pillars of global trade. It plays a critical role in the process from production point to consumption point of goods and services.

  • Global logistics market shows 5-7% annual growth
  • E-commerce growth increases logistics demand
  • Sustainable logistics solutions are gaining importance

Turkey Logistics Industry

Turkey is positioned as a strategic logistics center connecting Europe, Asia and Africa continents due to its geographical location.

  • Logistics sector constitutes 12% of Turkey's GDP
  • 2 million people are employed in the logistics sector
  • Importance increases further with the Silk Road project

Air Freight

The air cargo sector, preferred for fast and safe transportation, is of critical importance especially for high-value and urgent cargo.

  • Global air cargo traffic grows 4-5% annually
  • E-commerce increases air cargo demand
  • Turkey is a candidate country to become an air cargo hub

Sea Freight

90% of global trade is carried out by sea. It is the most economical transportation method for large volume and heavy loads.

  • Container traffic grows 3-4% annually
  • It is an environmentally friendly transportation method
  • Turkey's coastline to 3 seas provides strategic advantage

Road Transportation

Road transportation, which offers door-to-door delivery, is preferred especially for short and medium distance transportation.

  • 89% of freight transportation in Turkey is done by road
  • Offers flexible and fast delivery
  • Indispensable for last mile delivery

Warehousing and Distribution

Warehousing and distribution centers, which are the critical link of the modern logistics chain, play an important role in supply chain optimization.

  • Automation and robotic technologies are becoming widespread
  • Cold chain storage demand is increasing
  • Multi-channel distribution models are developing

Industry Trends and Future Predictions

Digitalization

IoT, AI and blockchain technologies are transforming the logistics sector.

Sustainability

Environmentally friendly transportation solutions and carbon footprint reduction efforts.

Automation

Autonomous vehicles and robotic systems are optimizing logistics operations.
